AGBOT

From Data to Harvest: Smarter Solutions for Farmers

Mission Statement

Our mission is to empower farmers and agricultural communities with intelligent, accessible, and sustainable technology that improves plant health monitoring and pest management. By combining artificial intelligence with mobile-first design, we aim to reduce crop losses, lower pesticide use, and promote food security.

Core Innovation

AGBOT pioneers a Damage-to-Insect Causality Engine that identifies specific insect species from leaf damage patterns. Unlike apps that only detect diseases, we answer the critical question: "What pest caused this damage?"

This enables targeted, IPM-compliant treatments that reduce pesticide misuse and support sustainable farming.
